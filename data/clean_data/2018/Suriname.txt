president, as it relates to our economic challenges and policy measures, my country as a commodity-based export economy, is highly vulnerable to commodity price shocks. the sharp downturn in gold and oil prices 3 years ago and the closure of the century-old bauxite operations in 2016 ha e led to a sharp recession and a severe loss in government revenues. the economy contracted by a combined 8% reliance on extractive industries; fiscal reforms aim at reducing reliance on direct tax income and broadening the tax base; and the establishment of a sovereign wealth fund will stabilize income
